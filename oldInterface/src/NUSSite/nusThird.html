The pay parking operating hours are shown below:

Saturday (excluding Public Holidays) 0830H – 1700H
Grace Period 10 mins for all vehicles

Visitors’ Parking

Visitors’ parking is available at car parks listed at Table 1. The car parks are installed with automated parking system using Electronic Road Pricing (ERP) technology. Car park access will be via in-vehicle unit (IU) identification for both season and short-term users. Visitors are reminded to park at the white lots as the red lots are strictly for staff season holders. The vehicles are subject to wheel-clamp for unauthorised parking if they are found parking at the red lots. The unclamping fee is $107.00.
